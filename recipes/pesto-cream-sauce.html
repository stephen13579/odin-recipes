Pesto Cream Sauce

Serve this amazing sauce over pasta and add some protein, such as chicken, if you wish!

  1. 3/4 cup heavy whipping cream
  2. 1/4 cup water
  3. 2 tablespon butter
  4. 1/4 cup prepated pesto sauce
  5. 1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
  6. 1/4 teaspoon salt
  7. 1/3 cup parmesan cheese, shredded or flakes (your preference!)
